About Us

In the first days of the russian-Ukrainian war, February 2022, we faced a number of problems where we felt the competence to find solutions.

We saw how problematic were the moments of supply of some medical equipment and medicines, and decided to take into our hands those issues. The biggest thing we are proud of is that we help only those who have a good reputation and are conscious people(partners, donors, aid receivers).
