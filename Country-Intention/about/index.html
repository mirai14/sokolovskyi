About Us

In the first days of the russian-Ukrainian war, February 2022, we faced a number of problems where we felt the competence to find solutions.

We saw how problematic were the moments of supply of some medical equipment and medicines, and decided to take into our hands those issues. The biggest thing we are proud of is that we help only those who have a good reputation and are conscious people(partners, donors, aid receivers).

Every single charity project is supervised by our team from the beginning till the last report is approved.

Our vision

Your first assistant in getting the necessary support.

Required support may lie in different directions: starting from consulting services and ending with direct aid (food, medicines provision, medical equipment supply and etc).

CO “ICF “Country Intention”” is a starting point to bring you to the desired result.
